# Team2
Team 2 AI prompts for the Plant AI study and SPSS outputs. 

If you want to look at the code for the Plant AI web-based simulator, go to: https://github.com/Wellesley-CS366/plantaistudy

## Prompt Generation.pdf
This shows what prompts we used to generate the multiple choice questions, answers, and AI responses (both authority and non-authority). 

## Raw Data.csv and Data Analysis.csv 
`Raw Data.csv` only shows the RAW data. `Data Analysis.csv` was cleaned via Google Sheets formula. You may access cleaning formula [in this link](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1otIp70Kvxn_IskSCytmB7aaUulkeN7j810iyHPDUhew/edit?usp=sharing). You must be logged into your Wellesley College email to access it. 

## SPSS Outputs
Contains `ANOVA Analysis.spv` and `T-test Analysis.spv`, our data analyses. All data were analysed in SPSS v29.
